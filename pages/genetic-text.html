遗传算法: Genetic Text

这是一段会进化的文本，它代表了一个国家的国王的染色体。这个国家由若干个地区组成，每个地区内最强的人成为这个地区的长官，最强的长官成为这个国家的国王。长官能够支配该地区所有的人，国王能够支配所有的长官。每个地区都有人口限制，地区之间有人口交换，越强的人能够产生越多后代，后代的染色体由上一代的染色体交叉互换和染色体突变产生。

This is a text that evolves, and this text represents the chromosome of the king of a country. The country consists of several regions, and the strongest person within each region becomes the governor of that region, with the strongest governor being the king of the country. The governor has dominion over all the people in the region, and the king has dominion over all the governors. Each region has a population limit and there are population exchanges between the regions, the stronger the person the more offspring he can produce, with the offspring having chromosomes created by chromosome crossovers and chromosome mutations from the previous generation.
