# 从零开始的光线追踪渲染器

::: warning 重要提醒

你也许想看的其实是这篇文章：[Taichi: 从零开始的光线追踪](taichi-ray-tracing.md)

A shader-based PBR ray tracing demo. Real-time denoise and you can freely adjust the camera's transforms, lens aperture, fov and focal length.
